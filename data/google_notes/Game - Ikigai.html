There main quest in life is to play in the now: ikigui

The boss is the illusion of reality. Believing we are the identities we inhabit. Getting through the event horizon. Unterthering. Seeing we are free to choose our identities.

The goal is to build towards the center
- What we love
- What we are good at
- What we can be paid for
- What the world needs

The main skill/knowledge trees to develop:
- communicating: language; reading; writing; symbolism
- calculating: numbers; math; stats; risk
- experimenting: science; process
- creating: art; music; pottery
- thinking: philosophy; models
- being: spirituality; virtue; religion

The character/role we chose to build is constantly able to be edited and changed towards a new path, always innovating and growing

The market/actors are able to identify the skills/knowledge trees they need/want making visible what characters need to do to develop

People and organizations are able to play a role in developing people, places, things that are what the world needs and what people can be paid for.

Leveling Up:
Education institutions, people, books, videos, podcasts, movies, teams, retreats, businesses provide skill building of general and increasingly specialized knowledge and skill.
- art, music, math, science, writing, reading
- philosophy, spirituality, ideas, beliefs
- communication, team work,
- play, strength, endurance, team building
- problem solving, resourcefulness

Lived experiences level up too. The activities completed on a resume that are self reported compared to the activities that are observed and reported by others
- soft skills
- hard skills
- outcomes

Individuals level up their character building potential. The potential increases as they complete side quests gain skills and knowledge. And as their real world experience is reported. An individual's skill tree is their lifelong resume showing the culmination of their XP and skill trees. This is captured in a block chain esque log and available via a digital or physical uncorruptible token.

The user value prop:
The individual's token can be matched with any other person or organization that is looking for those skills/XP/location.

The individual can more clearly see what skills and XP they need to get for their Ikigui and can be matched with complimentary teams that bring out the ikigui together.

If they have a missing skill or XP for a job or a project or a team, they can see that in their skill tree and XP. They can then access a comprehensive list of "quests" to complete to build those missing skills. This could include reading, listening, working, shadowing, etc.

People can be more free to find team members and partners with the skills they need to play their Ikigui

Organizations can better recruit people with the skills, passion and purpose to work in their Ikigui

People and institutions can better help teach and instruct people with the skills they need


What is the MVP?

One complete Ikigui profile
- a user token complete with skill tree and avatar
- a comprehensive skill tree for humanity
- open source data input
