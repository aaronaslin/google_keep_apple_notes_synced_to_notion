# AI Company  
  
  
The logic of chain-of-thought processing: chain together discrete steps that iterate on each other to build a final product.   
  
The ability to conceptualize this currently is at a task level and perhaps at a workflow level. However, a company creating a product is really just a string of chain-of-thought tasks executed together, sometimes in sequence, sometimes in parallel, and often with many iterative feedback loops. To get there one must either incrementally build replacements for existing tasks and workflows that culminate in a full replacement of human labor or, start from the desired outcome and build the company from scratch utilizing AI tools and agents.
